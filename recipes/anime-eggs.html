Anime Eggs

Description

These eggs inspired by the anime shokugei no soma are sure to leave you speechless, even if they don't destroy your clothes! Guaranteed to have great umami

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • rice
  • butter
  • chives
  • clothing you don't mind losing

Steps

  1. Cook rice
  2. Make an omelette with the eggs
  3. Cover the rice with the omelette and use what can only be described as magic to make it look fantastic
  4. Try to make sure your clothing doesn't explode
