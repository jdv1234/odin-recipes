Gun Ramen

Description

This action packed ramen recipe may not be delicious, but it sure packs a punch!

Ingredients

  • noodles
  • stock
  • garlic
  • 2 eggs
  • revolver

Steps

  1. Cook noodles
  2. Heat up stock and throw noodles inside of it to soak up juices
  3. Add garlic and any additional aromatics
  4. Slam that revolver into that beautiful mixture
